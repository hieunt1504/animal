THE ANIMAL

Herbivores

  • Eat plants only (leaves, grass, fruits)
  • Flat teeth for grinding food
  • Long digestive system
  • Eyes on the sides of the head (wide vision)
  • Often preyed upon by carnivores
  • Ruminants (e.g., cows) or hindgut fermenters (e.g., horses)
  • Chew slowly and repeatedly
  • Live in herds for protection

Carnivores

  • Eat meat (animals only)
  • Have sharp teeth and claws
  • Short digestive system
  • Eyes at the front of the head (for depth perception)
  • Strong jaw muscles
  • Fast and agile for hunting
  • Usually solitary hunters (e.g., tiger) or pack hunters (e.g., wolves)
  • Predator behavior
